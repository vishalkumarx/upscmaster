    • Social Factors: Alienation of tribal and marginalized communities, caste and communal tensions
    • Economic Impact: Reduced investment, disruption of local governance, increased militarization
    • Case Studies: Naxalism in Chhattisgarh, separatism in Northeast
    • Government Initiatives: Aspirational Districts Programme, Integrated Action Plan (IAP), Operation SAMADHAN
  • Insurgency and Naxalism
    • Naxalism: Origins (1967 Naxalbari uprising), ideology (Maoism), spread (Red Corridor)

    • Causes: Land alienation, resource exploitation, lack of political representation
    • Counter-Measures: Security operations (Greyhounds, CoBRA), development schemes (Bharatmala, PM-DevINE)
    • Challenges: Human rights concerns, collateral damage, local distrust
  • Terrorism
    • Forms: Cross-border (J&K), homegrown (urban terrorism), lone-wolf attacks
    • Key Groups: Lashkar-e-Taiba (LeT), Jaish-e-Mohammed (JeM), Indian Mujahideen
    • Causes: Radicalization, external state support (Pakistan), social media propaganda

    • Case Studies: 26/11 Mumbai attacks, Pulwama attack (2019)
  • Border Security
    • Challenges: Porous borders (Indo-Pak, Indo-Bangladesh), cross-border infiltration, smuggling

    • Security Forces: BSF, ITBP, Assam Rifles, role and mandate

    • Institutions: CERT-In, NCIIPC, Cyber Swachhta Kendra
    • Challenges: Encryption misuse, dark web, skill shortage in cybersecurity
